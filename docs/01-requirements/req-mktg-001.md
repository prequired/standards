# REQ-MKTG-001: Public Homepage

## Description

The system shall provide a public-facing homepage that communicates the agency's value proposition and drives visitor engagement.

## Rationale

The homepage is the primary entry point for prospects and the foundation of the marketing site.

## Source

- **Stakeholder:** Marketing Team
- **Document:** Marketing site requirements

## Fit Criterion (Measurement)

- Page loads in under 3 seconds
- Bounce rate under 50%
- Clear call-to-action above fold

## Dependencies

- **Depends On:** REQ-CMS-008 (static pages)
- **Blocks:** None
- **External:** None

## Satisfied By

- *To be linked after ADR creation*

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Hero section with headline, subhead, CTA
2. Services overview section
3. Portfolio/work samples section
4. Testimonials section
5. Blog highlights section
6. Contact/CTA section
7. Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
8. Fast loading with optimized images
9. Editable content via CMS
10. SEO optimized

## Notes

- Above-fold content critical for first impression
- Consider A/B testing different hero messages
- Integrate with analytics for conversion tracking
