# REQ-MKTG-003: Portfolio/Case Studies

## Description

The system shall showcase completed client work through a portfolio with detailed case studies.

## Rationale

Portfolio demonstrates capability, builds credibility, and helps prospects envision their project.

## Source

- **Stakeholder:** Marketing Team, Sales Team
- **Document:** Sales enablement requirements

## Fit Criterion (Measurement)

- Portfolio loads quickly with image optimization
- Case studies have structured format
- Portfolio filterable by category/service

## Dependencies

- **Depends On:** REQ-CMS-008 (pages), REQ-CMS-003 (media)
- **Blocks:** None
- **External:** None

## Satisfied By

- [ADR-0017: Marketing Site Architecture](../03-decisions/adr-0017-marketing-site-architecture.md)

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Portfolio gallery with thumbnails
2. Individual case study pages
3. Case study format: challenge, solution, results
4. Before/after images (if applicable)
5. Client testimonial in case study
6. Metrics/results highlighted
7. Technologies/services used
8. Filter by industry/service type
9. Client logo (with permission)
10. Related case studies

## Notes

- Get client approval before publishing
- Focus on measurable results when possible
- Consider password-protected case studies for NDAs
