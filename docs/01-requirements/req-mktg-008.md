# REQ-MKTG-008: Pricing Page

## Description

The system shall provide a pricing page that communicates service packages or pricing guidance.

## Rationale

Pricing information qualifies prospects, sets expectations, and reduces unqualified inquiries.

## Source

- **Stakeholder:** Sales Team
- **Document:** Sales enablement requirements

## Fit Criterion (Measurement)

- Pricing page reduces unqualified leads
- Clear path to inquiry/purchase
- Pricing updated as offerings change

## Dependencies

- **Depends On:** REQ-CMS-008 (static pages)
- **Blocks:** None
- **External:** None

## Satisfied By

- *To be linked after ADR creation*

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Display service packages/tiers
2. Feature comparison table
3. Clear pricing or "starting at" guidance
4. FAQ section addressing pricing questions
5. CTA to contact for custom quote
6. Toggle for monthly/annual (if applicable)
7. Enterprise/custom tier option
8. Common add-ons or upgrades
9. Money-back guarantee (if applicable)
10. Compare to competitors subtly

## Notes

- Consider whether to show exact pricing or ranges
- Test different pricing presentations
- Pricing page design impacts conversion significantly
