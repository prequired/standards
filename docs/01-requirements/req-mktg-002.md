# REQ-MKTG-002: Services Pages

## Description

The system shall provide dedicated pages for each service offering with detailed descriptions and calls-to-action.

## Rationale

Service pages target specific search queries, qualify prospects by interest area, and support the sales process.

## Source

- **Stakeholder:** Marketing Team, Sales Team
- **Document:** Service marketing requirements

## Fit Criterion (Measurement)

- Each service has dedicated landing page
- Service pages rank for relevant keywords
- Clear path to contact/inquiry

## Dependencies

- **Depends On:** REQ-CMS-008 (static pages)
- **Blocks:** None
- **External:** None

## Satisfied By

- *To be linked after ADR creation*

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Individual page per service
2. Service description and benefits
3. Process/approach explanation
4. Relevant portfolio examples
5. Service-specific testimonials
6. Pricing guidance (if applicable)
7. FAQ section
8. Clear CTA for inquiry
9. Related services links
10. Service category organization

## Notes

- Services: Web Design, Web Development, Branding, Marketing, etc.
- Consider service-specific lead magnets
- May link to case studies
